About Us

The Career Foundation Educational Academy (CFEA) is committed to empowering students through practical education and skill development. We collaborate with institutions like the Khwai Ihul Foundation to expand literacy and digital education across Manipur.

Our mission is to provide quality education that bridges the gap between academic learning and practical skills needed in today's competitive world.
